ABOUT US

⚭

McDonald's is an American hamburger and fast food restaurant. It was founded in 1940 by Richard and Maurice McDonald.
McDonald's confirmed plans to move its global headquarters to Chicago by early 2018.

Today, McDonald's is one of the world's largest restaurant chains. McDonald's primarily sells hamburgers, cheeseburgers,
chicken products, french fries, breakfast items, soft drinks, milkshakes, and desserts. The company has expanded its menu to include
salads, fish, wraps, smoothies, and fruit. McDonald's is the world's second largest private employer, 1.5 million work for franchises.
